FAQ

Commonly asked questions and answers

I can't find an article

This can simply be a lack of key words, our search function is advanced but some articles take precidence when a lack of identifying words are found. 

While we're constantly adding and improving our articles, sometimes we just haven't covered a topic yet. You can make a request via the contact forms however, if our understanding of the topic is limited it can take a while for us to write an article due to research and practice we take to develop a solid understanding.

How do I contact you?

You can find out more on our contact page

What do I do if I have found a problem?

Whether this be a typo in an article or a part of the website not working properly, please report this to us via the contact page and we will direct it to the appropriate person.

How do I share an article?

There are a couple ways to share an article. Currently, you can copy the URL or use the share button found on the articles.

I have knowledge and would like to share an article. Can I?

Yes! While we do not have accounts to which you can freely publish, we do accept written articles. To do this, you can go to the contact page and post a TEXT based version of your article. Please bear in mind that we use markdown to parse our articles from plain text to the page, so typically we ask for it in this format. We also have a few features to utilise to include images, automatic link adding and further. If you wish to learn more you can also ask for more information by visiting the contact us page.
